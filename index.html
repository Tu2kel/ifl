The Company

Who We Are

Imperio Federal Logistics is the active federal supply DBA of The House of Kel LLC — a Texas-based, veteran-owned contractor purpose-built for the DLA and state procurement lanes.

01 / Origin
Veteran-Founded

Founded by a 21-year U.S. Army veteran with a logistics and supply command background. Imperio applies that same operational discipline to the federal supply lane — clear processes, fast execution, no excuses.

02 / Posture
Built for Federal

SDVOSB and VetHUB-certified. CAGE 152U4. Active on DLA DIBBS, Texas ESBD, and SLED opportunities. CMMC Level 2 aligned to handle CUI workflows as defense procurement modernizes.

03 / Model
Asset-Light, Capital-Backed

No warehouse. No inventory drag. Imperio operates a tight loop between sourcing, bid, and award — backed by Factoring Express for PO funding and invoice factoring on every contract.

04 / Edge
Sourcing Network

Direct relationships with regional distributors and small manufacturers — not catalog resellers. Phone-first outreach turns every award into the start of a recurring supplier channel.

05 / Discipline
Margin-First Bidding

Every bid runs through a strict margin floor and BAA/TAA compliance gate. Imperio bids what pencils — and walks from what doesn't. Predictable execution beats opportunistic chasing.

06 / Footprint
Texas, National Reach

Headquartered in Killeen, Texas — adjacent to Fort Cavazos and the Central Texas defense corridor. Drop-ship capable to any government delivery address in the continental U.S.
